Purity Guaranteed

BIS Hallmarked Gold

Every gold piece carries the Bureau of Indian Standards hallmark — your guarantee of verified purity.

22K
916 Purity
91.6% pure gold — most popular for Indian jewellery. Rich lustrous finish, long-lasting. Ideal for necklaces, bangles and traditional bridal sets.
⬡ BIS 916 Certified
20K
833 Purity
83.3% pure gold — stronger than 22K, ideal for studded and intricate designs that need extra durability without sacrificing beauty.
⬡ BIS 833 Certified
18K
750 Purity
75% pure gold — perfect for diamond jewellery and daily-wear pieces. Scratch-resistant, modern and versatile for all lifestyles.
⬡ BIS 750 Certified
